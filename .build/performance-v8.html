What is a unique ad view?

A unique ad view is counted every time a new customer views your ad on the Zomato website or app within a day. It is also known as a unique impression.

How is a unique ad view measured?
Within a day (between 00:00 and 23:59), every new customer that views the ad is counted as a unique ad view. Even if a customer views your ad multiple times within the same day, they are still counted as one unique ad view.

Why do unique ad views matter?
More unique ad views mean greater number of customers are aware and exposed to your restaurant.

(CAPTION)In the image, each advertisement got an additional unique ad view from a customer in a day.

What is a unique ad click?

A unique ad click is counted every time a new customer clicks on your ad on the Zomato website or app within a day.

How is a unique ad click measured?
Within a day (between 00:00 and 23:59), every new customer that clicks on the ad is counted as a unique ad click. Even if a customer clicks your ad multiple times within the same day, they are still counted as one unique ad view.

Why do unique ad clicks matter?
A click means that a customer was attracted by your ad, wants to learn more about your restaurant, and has good probability to convert to business.

(CAPTION)In the image, when a unique customer taps on an ad on the Zomato app or clicks an ad on the Zomato website, it is counted as a unique ad click.

What is click through rate?

Click through rate (CTR)
Click through rate measures the effectiveness of your ad. It is a percentage displaying the unique customers who clicked on the ad compared to the unique customers who viewed the ad.

CTR = (Unique ad clicks) / (Unique ad views)

How can you increase your CTR?
Changing your banner images or banner text can increase your click through rate. Speak to your Sales representative at Zomato to make these changes.

What is the ad spend during this time?

This is the amount you are paying for all active ads on Zomato platform in the selected time period.

The ad spend is prorated for this time period and is derived from your total predicted ad spend for this campaign.

What is cost per thousand unique ad views?

Cost per thousand unique ad views is the cost of exposure for every 1000 unique ad views on the Zomato platform. It is another way to represent your ad spend in the selected time period.

Cost per thousand ad unique views = (Ad spend in selected time period) / (Unique ad views/1000)

What is cost per unique ad click?

Cost per unique ad click is the cost of every unique ad click on the Zomato platform. It is another way to represent your ad spend in the selected time period.

Cost per unique ad click = (Ad spend in selected time period) / (Total unique ad clicks)
