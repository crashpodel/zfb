What are unique ad views?

The number of unique people who viewed your ad within a day (between 00:00 and 23:59). Even if a person viewed your ad multiple times within the same day, it is still counted as one unique ad view.

Why do unique ad views matter?
 Unique ad views tell you how many unique people saw your ad and got to know about your restaurant.

What are unique ad clicks?

The number of unique people who clicked on your ad within a day (between 00:00 and 23:59). Even if a person clicked on your ad multiple times within the same day, it is still counted as one unique ad click.

Why do unique ad clicks matter?
 Unique ad clicks indicate the number of people who showed intent to visit or order from your restaurant.

Tip: Updated photos, menus, and information on your restaurant page leads to a better chance of this intent converting to business.

What is click through rate?

Click through rate (CTR)
Click through rate measures the effectiveness of your ad. It is a percentage displaying the unique customers who clicked on the ad compared to the unique customers who viewed the ad.

CTR = (Unique ad clicks) / (Unique ad views)

How can you increase your CTR?
Changing your banner images or banner text can increase your click through rate. Speak to your Sales representative at Zomato to make these changes.

Cost per unique ad view = (Ad spend in selected time period) / (Number of unique ad views)

What is cost per unique ad click?

Cost per unique ad click = (Ad spend in selected time period) / (Number of unique ad clicks)
